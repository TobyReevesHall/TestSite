About


UniBrowser is a web app produced by 7 first year students at The University of Manchester as the product for a first year project. It displays a student's timetable and upcoming deadlines, based on their tutorial group.

Why UniBrowser?


UniBrowser displays all of the information that a student needs for day-to-day work in the School of Computer Science, in a single page, rather than the existing system, which requires the use of multiple apps to see timetables and deadlines.

Developed by W4, The University of Manchester
